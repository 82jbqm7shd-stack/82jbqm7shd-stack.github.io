Mark Hopking is a British mariner whose professional life has encompassed senior command, and project leadership within the superyacht sector. He is the Baron of Balvill, and studies heraldry and genealogy, with particular interest in their historical and legal contexts.

Subdomains: Maritime career · Barony of Balvill · Heraldry · Genealogy

Maritime career

Hopking’s maritime career spans thirty years of expedition sailing, environmental & polar operations, and senior command within the superyacht sector. Later he has worked in project and programme leadership roles. Three new build projects have been delivered with benefit of Hopking's oversight. His strategic management has drawn him towards complex programs which require strong direction.

Barony of Balvill

Hopking is the Baron of Balvill, a dignity created by Crown charter in 1630. Following the Abolition of Feudal Tenure etc. (Scotland) Act 2000, the dignity of barony was formally severed from land ownership. It remains recognized in Scots law as a personal dignity, legally classified as incorporeal heritable property.
